Extra Info

  • There are certain collectibles that come in the form of cute plushies which add an adorable twist to such a violent game, showing off the Slayer's kind side.

  • Audio logs and text files also provide background information on the UAC, the demonic invasion, and the Slayer himself.

  • An example of said file includes the Slayer's Testament which recounts the history and legend of the Slayer from the moment he got his god-given abilities to the day he was put in the coffin he awakened from.
